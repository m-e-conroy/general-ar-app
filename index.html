How to Use

Getting Started

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Point your camera at a target image
  3. Keep the camera steady for best tracking
  4. 3D content will appear when target is detected

Target Images

For best results, use images with:

  • High contrast and distinct features
  • Good lighting conditions
  • Minimum size of 10cm when printed
  • Flat, non-reflective surface

Controls

Video: Use the bottom controls to play/pause and adjust volume

Recording: Tap the record button to capture your AR session

Settings: Customize tracking and display options
